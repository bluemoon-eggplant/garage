## Front Wheel Well Inner Fender Replacement (Both Sides)

Lifted the front end and removed the old inner fenders, then cleaned out the wheel wells.

<Image {...IMAGE_SIZES.FIXED.MDX_LG} src={Img1} alt="After removal" />

Installed the new ones. Clips were also brand new.  
However, possibly due to fender distortion, some clips couldn't be attached.

<Image {...IMAGE_SIZES.FIXED.MDX_LG} src={Img2} alt="After installation" />

## Added Screws to Left Door Inner Trim

The door trim had been floating loose for a while, so I removed it for inspection.  
Removing it wasn't too difficult, but the panel that hides the screws below the handle is held in with clips, and I scratched it with a flathead screwdriver while taking it off.  
Some of the screw mounting bosses were distorted, so I adjusted them before reassembly.

<Image {...IMAGE_SIZES.FIXED.MDX_LG} src={Img3} alt="Inside the door trim" />

While I was at it, I also added new sponge around the speakers since the old ones were falling apart.
