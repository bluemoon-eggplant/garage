## Drum Brake Maintenance

When I opened up the drum brake, the sides were worn in an odd pattern, suggesting it may have been operating improperly.

<Image {...IMAGE_SIZES.FIXED.MDX_LG} src={Img1} alt="Work in progress" />

Replaced both the cam and the shoes.

<Image {...IMAGE_SIZES.FIXED.MDX_LG} src={Img6} alt="Work in progress" />

## Rear Suspension Replacement

The original stock shocks that came with the bike had severe rust on the inside of the springs, so I swapped them out for new SR400 shocks.
Compared to the floaty ride of the originals, the new ones feel much firmer.
But it's by no means uncomfortable.
