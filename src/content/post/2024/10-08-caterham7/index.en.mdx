In conclusion, you shouldn't casually swap parts on this car.
The fitment is terrible, so modifications are a given.
This isn't just an aftermarket parts problem -- even OEM parts are a struggle.
To install the grille I purchased, I had to bend the grille's mounting brackets to fit the slots on the nose cone.
There are originally small brackets for grille mounting, but I also added zip ties to secure it firmly.
