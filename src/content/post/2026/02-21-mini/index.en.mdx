During the winter I had been running 20W-60, which is a pretty thick oil, and the engine felt heavy to start with noticeably more vibration.
Switching to 10W-40 made it a lot smoother — very happy with the change.
Also, I started buying oil in pail cans instead of 5L jugs, which is saving me some money.
